# Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)

## What is RAG?

Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) is an AI architecture that combines a Large Language Model (LLM) with an external knowledge base.

Instead of relying only on the model's internal knowledge, the system retrieves relevant information from trusted documents before generating a response.

---

## Why is RAG important?

Traditional AI assistants may generate incorrect or outdated information.

RAG helps improve:

- Accuracy
- Transparency
- Consistency
- Trustworthiness

---
